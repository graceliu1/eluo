the problem

The average woman in the US is exposed to 168 unique chemicals every day from personal care products alone.

Still, personal care products are largely an unregulated industry. Many of the harmful chemicals present in these products have not been tested for long-term effects on the human body.

Using unsafe shampoo, conditioner, and comestic products is dangerous. It's also difficult for the average consumer to check whether their products are safe. That's why we created Eluo.

Users can easily upload pictures of the ingredients label. Our system uses image to text OCR to check whether the chemicals are safe for use. We also provide recommendations for safer alternatives.

Use Eluo
the solution

Our web application accurately identifies ingredients from product labels. Then, chemicals are matched with our database and processed with a custom-trained NLP model to check whether they are safe for long-term use.

Users recieve notifications of their product safety status. Afterwards, they can receive custom recommendations for a variety of personal products like shampoo, conditioner, foundation, mascara, and more.

We've also created a model of a physical scanner prototype to be used in commercial centers. Users can scan labels and upload pictures on their phone to this web application.



the details

Web development: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript

Image-to-text processing: OCR, Tesseract OCR, PyTesseract, Python

Text processing: Natural language processing (NLP models), Java
