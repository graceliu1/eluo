the problem

The average woman in the US is exposed to 168 chemicals every day.

Personal care products are largely an unregulated industry. Many of the harmful chemicals present in these products have not been tested for long-term effects on the human body.

Using unsafe shampoo, conditioner, and comestic products is dangerous. It's also difficult for the average consumer to check whether their products are safe. That's why we created Eluo.

Users can easily upload pictures of the ingredients from the back . Our system uses image to text OCR to check whether the chemicals are safe for use. We also provide recommendations for safer alternatives.
